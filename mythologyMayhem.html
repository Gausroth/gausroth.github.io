Sluggerpunk

Mythology Mayhem follows several children from different ancient civilizations on their own journeys as they fight for their beliefs. After intervention from the gods of time and space, perspectives change between 2D and 3D, changing gameplay and keeping players on their toes.

Roles & Responsibilities

Initially I was brought on the Mythology Mayhem project to complete a level in the Norse chapter of the game. Right after I joined the project the lead programmer left to join a different project, and I was forced to modify scripts used in the first chapter to make the game playable. My responsibilities were not clear and since I was unable to test the Norse level my tasks were zero. As a team we decided to get the first Chapter working so I could at least work on the Norse levels. However, the game was showcased to the publisher, and they wanted the first chapter to be released as a demo. At this time my responsibilities were clear, get the first chapter polished and ready for release. As the sole full time programmer on the project I when through everything, fixed and updated most the scripts, reimported every image, recreated every 2D animation and even added 2D lighting to some of the levels.
