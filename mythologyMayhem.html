Research Summary

When I joined Mythology Mayhem, a lot was built but nothing was finished — key elements like sound and visual effects were missing. Audio and visuals are vital to accessibility: some players with visual or hearing impairments rely on complementary feedback to play. The game's audio also drives player immersion; absent or unrealistic sound creates a negative experience and breaks presence.
